Student Members: All Student Members of User-Centric Computing Group has carried a significant responsibility to contribute, manage and lead research activities. More specifically, student members are well-recognized and usually have achieved exceptional performance from Student Trainee status. We are extremely fortunate to have attracted and deem a great pleasure to have worked with several great students within User-Centric Computing Group.

Student Trainees: All Student Trainees of User-Centric Computing Group has also carried a strong responsibility to contribute relevant research activities. More specifically, student trainees are under the mentorship of specific student members, with regular synchronizations, meetings and seminars, to ensure their constant growth and focus on research. We have experienced several trainees and we are glad to have them with us.

Research Affiliates: All Former Student Members of User-Centric Computing Group are free to continute being affiliated, by taking the responsibility to lead relevant projects, mentor current student members and coordinate research progress with the current Project Manager.
